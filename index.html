PASTRIES

I love eating them. This is a webpage about my favorite pastries, and ones that I think are cool. Have fun!

European -

In all of Europe, France is probably most well-known for its pastries. Some of them are macarons, pain au chocolat, and crepes. But there is more to Europe than France. I also think cannolis, danishes, and spanakopita are pretty cool.

Asian -

I know a lot of pastries from just growing up in a Chinese household. My favorite by far are cong you bing, or scallion pancakes. Mooncakes are amazing, too. Though not from mainland China, feng li su (pineapple cakes) is great. Some other interesting ones are Japanese taiyaki, curry puffs, and jalebi.

American -

It's commonly said that American food is just a mix of other cuisines, but there are some pastries that have become important parts of American cuisine. For example the pies (apple, pumpkin, and pecan). Although I am not a fan, bear claws do exist too. I guess donuts count as well, although the cronut was created in New York.
